So'GO (Service on the Go)

About the Business

Business Name and Logo

The name of the business Service on the Go (So’GO) exactly implies what the business is offering. Delivery and on-call workers will be the trademark of the company, and it is a type of service that the business specializes in. The business is inspired by food delivery services. The company aims to create a brand that is easy to use and that enables people from all around the world to try our services. The company’s logo is designed to look simple and appropriate for its name. In the middle is the motorcycle, which represents what the business offers, and the tiny stainless steel cloche dome at the top of the motorcycle symbolizes the other services provided by the business. One of the business’s goals is to help make the customer's lives easier.

Background of the Industry


Service on the Go is the country’s first-ever all-around online service-providing platform that fulfills your everyday on-the-go needs. It is designed to be highly adaptable, providing a wide range of service categories and a selection of service offerings. It is the perfect place for everyone looking for individualized on-demand services, as well as for freelancers and contractors who are trying to grow their client base, due to its wide range of possibilities. In today's world, time is a precious commodity, and So'Go recognizes the value of efficiency. By utilizing the power of technology, our website offers the process of finding, booking, and managing services that allows customers to look for the service that they want. Services are to be delivered to their customers without the hassle of going out.
